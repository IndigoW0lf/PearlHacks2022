FINDING EMPLOYMENT

Finding employment can be tough, especially for marginalized groups.

Less than 1 in 5 employees in the hi-tech industry are women, even though women make up more than 50% of the US workforce.

"How do I even start?"
"What if the standard way into tech doesn't feel right to me?"
"Will this employer be right for me?"

There are so many paths to explore when it comes to employment. Do you want to forgo a higher salary for a more equitable and inviting work environment? Starting with a list of what's important to you specifically helps to approach the daunting task of finding a job that's right for you. From finding places to apply to, to perfecting your interview process- there are vast resources out there to place you in the perfect position.

Don't let the hiring process overwhelm you- you've got this!
